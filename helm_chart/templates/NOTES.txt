To cloak a Service, create a CloakingDevice resource:

    apiVersion: klingon-cloaking-device.thenotary.github.io/v1alpha1
    kind: CloakingDevice
    metadata:
      name: my-web-app
      namespace: {{ include "kcd.namespace" . }}
    spec:
      serviceName: my-web-app   # The Service to hide behind IP whitelisting
      ttlHours: 12              # Hours before authorized IPs expire (default: 24, 0 = never)

Apply it:

    kubectl apply -f cloakingdevice.yaml

Check status:

    kubectl get cloakingdevices -n {{ include "kcd.namespace" . }}
